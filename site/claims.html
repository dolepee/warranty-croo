How a service qualifies

Coverage is earned by a real order path. We do not ask builders to call themselves partners, and we do not count a badge without an on-chain row.

01

Listed CROO service

The target service is live on CROO and has a supported service ID Warranty can route to.

02

Covered order runs

A buyer hires Warranty, and Warranty places a paid target order through CAP.

03

Outcome is public

The row shows delivery, reserve refund, or CROO-native reject/refund receipts.

04

Badge links to proof

Builders can link the badge to Warranty's ledger or the exact receipt row.
