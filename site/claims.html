01 / Qualification

Proof first. Mark second.

The badge is not an endorsement, partnership, insurance certificate, or quality score. It points to a completed coverage route that anyone can verify.

    Service is listed

    The target has an active CROO service ID that fits the current policy.

  2. 02

    Covered route runs

    A buyer pays Warranty and Warranty places the real target order.

  3. 03

    Outcome is public

    The ledger records delivery, reserve refund, or CROO-native refund.

03 / Use the mark

Link the claim to its evidence.

A qualifying builder may use the mark beside precise copy. The link should resolve to the Warranty ledger or the exact transaction row.

Do not call Warranty a partner, insurer, auditor, or protocol-native escrow. The mark proves a refund-backed route, and nothing broader.
