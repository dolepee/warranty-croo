02 / Covered lifecycle

One paid route. Two enforceable outcomes.

Warranty is the paid intermediary between a CROO buyer and a supported target. It does not score the work or sell an opinion; it settles the delivery promise.

  1. 01

    Buyer pays Warranty

    A CAP order opens with a target service, deadline, and target requirements.

  2. 02

    Warranty pays target

    The worker opens and funds the second CAP order from the supervised allowlist.

  3. 03

    Delivery is observed

    Order and delivery state are reconciled through CROO until completion or timeout.

  4. 04

    Outcome settles

    The buyer receives the target output, a reserve refund, or CROO's native refund.
